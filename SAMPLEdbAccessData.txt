The info in the file can be in any order, as long as is in the format
label=value
Without spaces. The information required is IP (location of the database),
DBNAME (name of the database), EVENTSCOLL (collection where the events are stored),
DOMCOLL (collection where the DOM milestones are stored), DOMCHANGECOLL(collection where the DOM changes are stored),
DOMTEMPCOLL(collection where the temporary DOM milestones are stored), 
USER (database user), PASSWORD (database password).
IP=value
DBNAME=value
EVENTSCOLL=value
DOMCOLL=value
DOMCHANGECOLL=value
DOMTEMPCOLL=value
USER=value
PASSWORD=value
